DRUGGS Ghana — Organisation Overview & Policies

Registered base: Accra, Ghana • Last updated: 2025

Who we are

DRUGGS Ghana (Dedicated to Rational Use of Generics and Global Standards) is a passion-driven, youth-led non-profit organisation focused on improving medication safety, promoting rational drug use, preventing substance misuse and advancing health equity across Ghana.

Mission

Advance health equity by ensuring reliable access to safe, effective and affordable medicines while promoting rational use, preventing substance misuse and delivering evidence-based interventions aligned with global standards.

Core Pillars

  • Access to Essential Medicines
  • Rational Drug Use
  • Harm Reduction & Safety
  • Research & Policy Advocacy
  • Public Health Education
