Passion-driven, youth-led action for safer medicines and healthier communities

DRUGGS Ghana (Dedicated to Rational Use of Generics and Global Standards) brings together early-career public-health professionals, pharmacists and community advocates to advance rational drug use, harm reduction, drug safety and research across Ghana.

View governance & policies

Core Programmes

Rational Drug Use

Prescriber training, stewardship tools, and community education aligned to WHO guidance.

Harm Reduction & Safety

Youth-focused prevention, referrals, and harm-minimising community outreach.

Access to Essential Medicines

Advocacy for quality generics and partnerships to strengthen local supply chains.

Research & Policy

Operational research to inform national policy and practice; monitoring & evaluation.

Public Health Education

School curricula, peer-education networks and culturally appropriate media campaigns.

Community Empowerment

Capacity building and community advisory structures to ensure local ownership.

Our Approach

We combine community engagement, youth leadership and scientific rigour. Projects are co-designed with community stakeholders, measured for impact, and governed transparently.
